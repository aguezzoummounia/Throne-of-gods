# Product Overview

**Throne of Gods** is an interactive fantasy web experience centered around the fictional world of Erosea. The application features:

- An immersive hero section with WebGL animations and custom shaders
- A personality quiz that matches users with villain characters
- Character profiles with detailed backstories, powers, and stats
- An interactive map of the fantasy world
- Rich multimedia content including background music and sound effects
- SEO-optimized pages with structured data for discoverability

The primary user journey involves exploring the fantasy world and taking a quiz to discover which villain character aligns with their personality, with a call-to-action to watch a trailer.
